Swedish Aloo Gobi Curry

Description

Aloo Gobi inspired curry. Not sure what makes it Swedish other than I...

Ingredients

  • 4 potatoes (diced)
  • 2 onions (diced)
  • 1/2 a head of cabbage (chopped)
  • Garlic (zest)
  • 1 carrot (diced)
  • Ginger (grated)
  • 1 pear (peeled and diced) - (or more?)
  • A handful of Green peas
  • Vinegar
  • Tomato puree
  • 3 dl cream
  • Soy sauce
  • Spices:
    • Garam masala
    • Cayenne pepper (careful...)
    • Cinnamon
    • Cardemom
    • Allspice

Instructions

  1. Caramelize the onions and cabbage (let it take time) in a big pot
  2. Boil the potatoes until almost cooked through
  3. Add the pear, carrot, ginger, garlic, and spices to the pot and for a few minutes
  4. Add tomato puree
  5. Deglaze with vinegar and a bit of water
  6. Add soy sauce
  7. Add potatoes and peas and let simmer for a few minutes
  8. Serve with rice
