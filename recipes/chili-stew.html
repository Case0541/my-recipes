Chili Stew

Description

This is a simple and delicious chili stew that's great for a cold, lazy Sunday. Perfect meal prep for the week!

Ingredients

  • 1 kg ground beef or prime rib (roughly chopped)
  • 3 onions (diced)
  • Garlic cloves (minced)
  • 2 red bell peppers (diced)
  • Chilis according to taste (diced)
  • 2 tomatoes (diced)
  • Chipotle paste
  • Tomato puree
  • Beef broth
  • Dark beer
  • Root vegetables, for example:
    • 2 carrots (diced)
    • 1 potato (diced)
    • 2 sweet potatoes (diced)
    • 2 parsnips (diced)
  • 2 packages of black beans (drained and rinsed)
  • Spices:
    • Oregano
    • Cumin
    • Cilantro/Coriander
    • Cocoa powder
    • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Brown the meat in a large oven-safe pot
  2. Add the onions, garlic, and chilies and cook for a few minutes
  3. Add the bell peppers and tomatoes for a few minutes more
  4. Add spices and tomato puree
  5. Deglaze with beer
  6. Add broth and chipotle paste
  7. Let simmer in the oven for 1-2 hours (3-4 hours if prime rib is used). Stir occassionally and add water if needed
  8. Add root vegetables for the last hour of cooking
  9. Add the black beans for the last 30 minutes of cooking
  10. Salt and pepper to taste and serve with rice and sour cream (or however else you want)!
